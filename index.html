Frequently Asked Questions

How does Junto notify me when friends are nearby?

Junto sends you a simple notification when friends in your selected groups are close to your current location. It's not about live tracking; it's about helping you catch spontaneous opportunities.

Does Junto track my live location all the time?

No, Junto does not track your live location. We prioritize your privacy. The app only uses location data to send notifications when you're near your friends, and we don't store real-time location info.

Can I choose who gets notified when I'm nearby?

Absolutely! You can create different groups within the app and set specific preferences for each group. You control who sees your status.
