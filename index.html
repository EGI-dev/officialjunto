Why Junto?

Privacy-first by design

No background tracking. Location is shared only in a time-boxed post you create. Posts auto-expire and disappear.

Spontaneous, not stressful

No feeds, no follower counts, no addictive loops. Junto nudges you off-screen and into the real world.

Simple and clear

Create a plan, tag a venue, set an expiry. Friends tap Join and get a Maps deep-link, with optional ETA.

Privacy & Safety

Your data, under your control

  • No background location: we access location only when you post.
  • Time-boxed sharing: each post auto-expires.
  • Clear visibility controls: friends and chosen circles only.
  • No infinite feeds. No ads. No dark patterns.

Safety tools

  • Easy reporting for content or user behavior.
  • Block and remove features to keep your space safe.
  • Venue-partner escalation for onsite incidents.
  • We review safety reports with care and urgency.

FAQ

Is Junto free? +
Yes during the alpha. We're exploring fair, transparent pricing for future venue-powered perks—never pay-to-be-seen social feeds.
Do you track my location in the background? +
No. We only get location when you choose to post a plan. After expiry, it's gone from your feed.
Who can see my posts? +
Only your chosen circles—friends and the people you share with. Junto avoids public feeds by default.
Where will you launch first? +
We're starting at Brussels universities, then expanding city by city based on demand.
