FAQ

How does Junto notify me?

Junto sends a simple notification when friends in your selected groups are close to your current location. It's not about live tracking; it's about catching spontaneous opportunities.

Does Junto track my live location 24/7?

No. We prioritize your privacy. The app only uses location data to send notifications when you're near friends. We don't store or share real-time location history.

Can I control who sees me?

Absolutely. You create different groups and set specific preferences for each. You control who sees your status and when they get notified.
